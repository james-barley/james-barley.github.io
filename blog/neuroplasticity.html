Mindset

  • Discuss what it is and why it is relevant

    It is the ability to be confident and positive when overcoming obstacles you may face when learning something new or developing a new skill. Growth mindsets understand the importance of persistence and determination. By changing the way you think, you can change the way you learn

  • In this exploration, did anything surprise you? Change for you?

    I have had past experience of failing to learn because of my mindset and see how valuable growth mindset is when learning. I think what changed is not being so hard on myself when I am slow to learn new things.

  • How will you integrate growth mindset into your learning journey?

    Be confident that it will come and give myself more grace when the learning doesn’t come quickly or as fast as I’d like it.
